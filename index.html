MostlyPeacefulNewsSpeak.js

Drag this link to your bookmarks bar to convert any page to 2020 media acceptable phrasing*: MostlyPeacefulNewsSpeak

*Events are considered mostly peaceful if less than 51% of the Earth's surface is covered in violence related to the event and less than 51% of the Earth's population is a direct casualty of the event.

Try it out on topics like the Battle of the Somme.

Works in Firefox. Not currently working on Chrome or IE.
