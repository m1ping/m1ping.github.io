MostlyPeacefulNewsSpeak.js

Save this link to your bookmarks to convert any page describing an event* to 2020 media acceptable phrasing: MostlyPeacefulNewsSpeak

*Events are considered mostly peaceful if less than 51% of the Earth's surface is covered in violence directly related to the event and less than 51% of the Earth's population is a direct casualty of the event.

Works in Firefox. Not currently working on Chrome or IE.
